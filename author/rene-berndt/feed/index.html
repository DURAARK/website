DURAARK will present the ongoing research on  long term archival of architectural data at the conference “Digitale Bibliothek” (Digital Library) in Graz, Austria on the Februar 23rd, 2015. The upcoming conference is dedicated to the motto “Unser digitales Gedächtnis” (Our Digital Memory). With lectures, workshops, a poster and commercial exhibition, the conference is an established forum for discussing specific requirements that should be considered in the design and establishment of modern online knowledge repository.

The conference is organized by the “Austrian Centre for Digital Humanities, University of Graz” and  “Europeana Local Austria”. Check out the conference website for details and updates: http://conference.ait.co.at/digbib/index.php/digbib2015/digbib2015
