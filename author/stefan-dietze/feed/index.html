Stefan Dietze – DURAARK http://duraark.eu Durable Architectural Knowledge Thu, 17 May 2018 15:19:12 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.4.23 DURAARK and Digital Reconstruction http://duraark.eu/duraark-and-digital-reconstruction/ http://duraark.eu/duraark-and-digital-reconstruction/#respond Mon, 23 Mar 2015 19:49:26 +0000 http://duraark.eu/?p=1428 Last week, DURAARK coordinator Stefan Dietze participated in two workshops organised by the Herder Institute in Marburg, Germany. A workshop on 17 March, attended by roughly 60 experts from architecture, historical sciences and computer sciences, was dedicated to the use of semantic web and linked data in the context of “digital reconstruction”, i.e. the construction of 3D models and related contextual data about cultural heritage buildings from sparse and incomplete information sources. Here, works from affiliated projects and working groups were presented and discussed, where several apparent links with DURAARK activities emerged.

This event was followed by a second workshop on 18 March on the topic of Virtual Research Environments in related cultural heritage and digital reconstruction settings, with a strong emphasis on the use of linked data principles. Stefan Dietze presented, amongst others, recent DURAARK research and results (slides), which have been met with great interest by the audience and have paved the way for further exchange, for instance, of data, vocabularies and knowledge and further collaboration as part of the digital reconstruction working group.

 

]]> http://duraark.eu/duraark-and-digital-reconstruction/feed/ 0 DURAARK explores Semantic Digital Archives http://duraark.eu/duraark-explores-semantic-digital-archives/ http://duraark.eu/duraark-explores-semantic-digital-archives/#respond Thu, 29 Aug 2013 14:29:10 +0000 http://duraark.eu/?p=568 DURAARK partners will attend the 17th International Conference on Theory and Practice of Digital Libraries (TPDL) and present an early roadmap and some initial results related to the DURAARK Semantic Digital Archive during the collocated 3rd International Workshop on Semantic Digital Archives (SDA). The SDA2013 workshop scope – “shifting the focus of (traditional) digital archives to knowledge representation and management issues surrounding digital preservation” – is a natural fit with the DURAARK activities on exploiting Web data and knowledge, in particular Linked Data, for enriching architectural models and data. The DURAARK paper, co-authored by partners from the L3S Research Center, TU Eindhoven, Bonn University and Fraunhofer Austria, will introduce the most recent activities and future plans for building a semi-automatically curated archive of architectural Web knowledge.

TPDL2013 will take place in Valetta, Malta between 22-26 September and we are anticipating an interesting conference and workshop.
