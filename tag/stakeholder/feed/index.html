November 2014 – Copenhagen – Presentations Online http://duraark.eu/sustainable-building-information-workshop-12-november-2014-copenhagen-presentations-online/ http://duraark.eu/sustainable-building-information-workshop-12-november-2014-copenhagen-presentations-online/#respond Mon, 17 Nov 2014 22:56:44 +0000 http://duraark.eu/?p=1262 The Sustainable Building Information Workshop  on 12. November 2014 gathered practitioners, researchers, software developers and members of the archival community in order to discuss the ongoing shift in the operation, design, documentation and construction of buildings. Here formerly separated working practices merge to a digital lifecycle of building information. more information on the background and motivation of the day here.

The event was structured around a set of lectures that introduced the currents state and questions in research and practice – visited through the perspectives of institutional building owners, Facility management software developers and the DURAARK project. The day presented for the later a great opportunity to receive stakeholder feedback on the currents state of the project. For this we want to thank every participants for their valuable time and feedback.

On this page we want to document the participating persons and institution, as the slides of our dear presenters.
